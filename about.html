About

This is the official Wolaita Sodo University Student Result Viewing Website. It is designed to give students easy and secure access to their academic records, including course registrations and examination results.

Students can log in using their university ID and password to view their profile, check registered courses by year and semester, and see detailed exam results including test scores, assignment marks, final exam scores, and overall total out of 100.

This portal is managed by the Registrar's Office of Wolaita Sodo University. All data is kept secure and only accessible to the registered student. For any issues with your results or login, please contact the Registrar's Office directly.
